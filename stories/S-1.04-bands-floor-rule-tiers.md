# S-1.04: Bands, Floor Rule & Effective Tiers

## Narrative
- **As a** CISO reading the headline, **I want** a maturity band that can never average away a missing critical control, **so that** the number survives my skepticism.

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC-001 (traces to BC-4.04.003 postconditions 1–3)
- Band derivation: positional partition of [0,1] by N−1 thresholds, boundary belongs to higher band; result carries `band` and `band_uncapped`; undefined percent → no band.
- **Test:** `test_BC_4_04_003_band_derivation_boundaries()` + Kani VP-046/VP-047

### AC-002 (traces to BC-3.03.004 postcondition 2 — resolution function only)
- `effective_tier(goal, overrides)` = active override else catalog default (override records come from op-store later; pure resolution lives here).
- **Test:** `test_effective_tier_resolution()` (proptest VP-035)

### AC-003 (traces to BC-4.04.004 postconditions 1–3)
- Floor rule: gating set = {effective Critical ∧ level ∈ {NI, Unknown}}; non-empty ⇒ band = min(uncapped, second-lowest band), `floor_rule_triggered=true`, exhaustive `critical_gaps[]` with tier source; empty ⇒ uncapped; percent never altered.
- **Test:** `test_BC_4_04_004_floor_rule()` + Kani VP-048/VP-049/VP-050

### AC-004 (traces to BC-4.04.004 invariant 3, EC-003, EC-004)
- Critical at PI/LI does not gate; Critical at N/A does not gate (and is excluded from percent); all-critical-all-unknown triggers with band already Foundational (cap no-op, flag still true).
- **Test:** `test_BC_4_04_004_gating_boundaries()`

### AC-005 (traces to BC-4.04.004 canonical vectors)
- All four canonical vectors pass, incl. 33-FI + 1-critical-NI ⇒ uncapped Resilient, final Developing.
- **Test:** `test_BC_4_04_004_canonical_vectors()`
