# S-4.03: Tier Override Lifecycle

## Narrative
- **As a** site that knows its context, **I want** to reclassify a goal's criticality with a recorded reason, **so that** the floor rule fits reality without becoming gameable.

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC-001 (traces to BC-3.03.004 postconditions 1–3)
- `record_override`: tier ∈ {critical, high, standard}; non-empty trimmed rationale required (E-VAL-008); at most one active per goal, supersede-with-history (append-only like snapshots); `--clear` restores default as a recorded, rationale'd event; existing snapshots' cached Scores untouched.
- **Test:** `test_BC_3_03_004_lifecycle()` (VP-035 effective-tier proptest already in S-1.04 — extend with history)

### AC-002 (traces to BC-3.03.004 EC-001/EC-002, DEC-005)
- Demoting a floor-gating goal is permitted; the disclosure obligation flows to every band surface (VP-036 — asserted in S-5.x snapshot tests; this story provides the disclosure data); same-tier-as-default override accepted and recorded.
- **Test:** `test_BC_3_03_004_demotion_disclosure_data()`

### AC-003 (traces to BC-3.03.004 EC-003/EC-004)
- Whitespace-only rationale → E-VAL-008, nothing recorded; overrides on goals dropped by later migration are preserved + reported (integration with S-3.04 orphan handling).
- **Test:** `test_BC_3_03_004_validation()`
